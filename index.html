    Hammoq

    The Hammoq app helps resellers get their listing done faster and easier so that they can focus on making more money. The aim is for users to only have to submit pictures and a few details about the product and then Hammoq's machine learning will fill in the necessary product details. The app can also crosslist users' products across multiple marketplaces

    I helped build this app from idea to MVP and then to what it is today. The co-founders gave me vague ideas of what they wanted and, using my iOS dev skills, I helped turn it into a working product.

    SmartBeat

    This is the app that I helped build for a startup. I worked on the front end using the Flutter framework. This app is designed to show the heartrate of a baby including other related features. This app is available on the Apple App store and the Google Play Store. It works in tandem with an IR camera and remote basestation to communicate with.

    AutonoDrone

    This is my most recent project. It utilizes the DJI-iOS-Mobile SDK to provide functions for flying their drones.
